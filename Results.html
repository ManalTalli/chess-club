Take Part in Tournaments

Our chess club is one of the best in New York, and over the years of our existence, we have taught several thousand people to play. Our tutors have willingly shared, and continue sharing their knowledge and skills.

Our club has trained 23 champions from various US states. Another 5 of our students became US champions, as well as winners of major tournaments in North and South America. Our students also took part in world championships. Great experience and love of chess unite us and help to achieve success!
