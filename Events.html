EVENTS

Don't Miss Our Events!

Our school regularly holds various events. You cannot pass them by for three reasons!

01

Our lecturers and invited champions will provide you with a wealth of useful information. When attending our events, you will learn everything you've always wanted to know about the world of chess.

02

You will increase your sills. You will be able to learn certain techniques and tactics that will help you in the game. We offer only useful knowledge and lots of practice with real chess champions. 

03

Meet the star! We regularly invite various celebrities from the world of chess. Legends such as Garry Kasparov, Vladimir Kramnik, Viswanathan Anand, Magnus Carlsen have visited our club. 

Events

Our chess club is open to everyone! We often hold free events for those who want to get acquainted with the fascinating world of chess, meet other players, and improve their skills.

12 AUG, 12:30 PM Paul Foster
Lecture on Game Strategy

This lecture will be useful for both beginners and experienced players. You will learn how to choose a strategy when playing against a particular opponent, what are the strategies, what tactics are chosen in this or that case.

18 AUG, 5:00 PM Ann King
Introduction to the Game of Chess

Do you want to learn how to play chess? Then be sure to visit our open master class for beginner chess players. Our tutors will give you the basics of the game of chess. You also can meet our teachers and other members of our club.

28 AUG, 2:30 PM Veselin Topalov
Master-class by Veselin Topalov

Save the date to see the game of one of the best chess players of our time. Veselin Topalov will share his experience, show several of his signature moves and techniques, thanks to which he could become a two-time world champion.

28 AUG, 05:00 PM Veselin Topalov
Autograph Session With Veselin Topalov

After the master class, you can have a talk with the world chess champion! Veselin Topalov will hold an autograph session, and you will get the opportunity to take a picture with one of the most famous chess players in history.
